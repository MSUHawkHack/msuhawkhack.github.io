About

HawkHack is an intense Hackathon that unites many different students like software developers, graphic designers and more at all skill levels. It's a learning event for students who will be working alongside industry experts and professionals hosting seminars during the event. Students will work in groups and compete to win a prize and further their computer knowledge while having fun doing it! We expect more than double the 2020 attendance for this upcoming HawkHack.

Theme

This year's hackathon theme is Hackprecon, and it promises to be an exciting and memorable event. The hackathon will have a St. Patrick's Day theme, with challenges and games for participants to enjoy. We can't wait to see what creative ideas and solutions the participants come up with during this year's hackathon and as always, Good Luck!!

Register For HawkHack

Signing up for this hackathon is easy! You can register as an individual or as part of a team of up to four people. The hackathon is open to high school and college students who are currently enrolled and active in their studies. If you are under 18 years old, a parent or guardian will need to sign a permission slip, which will be emailed to you after you register. Once you have completed the registration process, you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours with your participant number, team number, and a link to join the hackathon's Discord server. This email will also contain any further instructions you need to know in order to prepare for the event. We can't wait to see you at the hackathon!

FAQ

When and Where is HawkHack?

HawkHack 2023 will take place MArch 18 & March 19th 2023 in-person at the Student Center Ballrooms!!


Do I need a team?

Nope! You're more than welcome to register as a team or by yourself. You can also find a team at the event and join then.


Will there be food and activities?

Yes and yes! We eill have free food, free swag from sponsors, tech and career workshops, and other fun events (like bingo and karaoke)!


What should I bring?

Bring a form of identification, and whatever you need to hack. This includes your laptop, your charger, and any hardware you'll need for your project.


Who can sign up and compete?

Any undergraduate or graduate student at any college in the US, or any recent (within 6 months) graduate from a college, as well as current high school students may register. Note: Participants under the age of 18 will need a parent/gaurdian to sign some forms!!


Can I use olf projects/code?

NO! The point of a hackathon is to build something original in a short time frame. however you are allowed to use any open-source frameworks and third-party APIs, with proper citations of course.


Will there be prizes?

YES! We have many prizes ranging from swag all the way to airpods and a monitor and some special prizes from sponsors. We have many catagories for prizes and many opportunities to win something!!


Do I have to pay to compete?

Nope, there is no out of pocket cost for you to sign up or compete. Please not we do not off travel reimburstments at this time.


Will there be parking?

Yes, free overnight parking will be located at (talk to parking services)


What shouldn't I bring?

We ask that you not bring your desktop or any other non-portable hardware to HackNJIT, as we have limited space and it is hard to rearrange tables to accommodate everyon brining their own. Of course, weapons, firearms, alcohol, and illegal drugs are strictly forbidden. If you can't take it on a flight, you can't take it to HawkHack!
